---

## What it does

Verify Backlinks™ analyzes backlinks directly on the page and detects:

- whether the backlink actually exists (or is missing)
- follow type (dofollow, nofollow, no link, error)
- risk label (clean, gambling, adult, drugs)
- toxic score (0–100 SEO risk)
- clear explanation of risk signals
- placement and placement detail
- topical relevance
- manipulation patterns and PBN footprints
- language mismatches and unnatural placements
- indexing recommendation (Index / Noindex / Consider disavow)
- canonical, noindex and robots issues

## Why this matters

Backlink tools often rely on large databases.

- links that don’t exist anymore are still counted

- context is missing  

Verify Backlinks™ works differently:

- every backlink is checked on the live URL  
- missing and broken links are detected

- decisions are based on real page data

Verify Backlinks™ supports two types of backlink audits:

- **Pre-index backlink audits**  
  Evaluate backlinks before they are indexed to prevent low-quality or manipulative links from affecting rankings.

- **Post-index backlink audits**  
  Analyze existing backlinks to identify risks, clean up link profiles and improve overall backlink quality.
